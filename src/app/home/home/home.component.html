The study of martial arts offers several unique advatages to the physical fitness of the student. The typical training provides the following health benefites:

  • Helps the lungs operate more efficiently.
  • Enlarges the blood vessels, making them more pliable and reducing the resistance to blood flow, this lowering the diastolic blood pressure.
  • Increases the blood supply, especially red blood cells and hemoglobin.
  • Makes the body tissue healthier by supplyig it with more oxygen.
  • Conditions the heart, providing more reserve for emergencies.
  • Promotes better sleep and relaxation.
  • Provides muscular strength.
  • Flexiblity of joints, muscles and ligaments.

Martial arts offers a super means of developing the characteristics of a good performance in other areas:

  • Dynamic energy-the ability to give 110% for long periods of time.
  • Ability to quickly change direcion of movement.
  • Ability to control ones limbs with precise movements.
  • Excellent peripheral vision.
  • The ability to block out and avoid distractions.
  • Ability to better control ones behavior and emotions.
  • Ability to push oneself further than thought possible.
  • The right to be an Individual.
  • The importance of teamwork and respect for others.
