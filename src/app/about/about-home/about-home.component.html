ChunJiDo

Founded by Grandmaster Robert Cutrell in 1996, ChunJiDo International is one of the world's most prestigious martial arts organizations. Originally developed as the governing body for the art of ChunJiDo, the organization has now grown to not only include the global practitioners of ChunJiDo, but also affiliates in 5 continents and over 30 countries! ChunJiDo International is committed to providing the highest quality of physical and mental martial arts & self-defense training in the art of ChunJiDo, and affiliation with a worldwide family of diverse martial arts systems from around the globe. Welcome! I am Master Heather Coffman, owner and instructor of Coffman's Martial Arts Academy. I am excited to be able to share my knowledge and love of ChunJiDo and Tae Kwon-Do with others. I trained and was promoted under Grand Master Thomas Black. I am currently studying under ChunJiDo founder Grand Master Robert Cutrell and am the State Director of Chunjido International.


I have been training in the martial arts for 16+ years. In that time, I have earned a 6th degree black belt in Tae Kwon-Do and a 3rd degree black belt in ChunJido. During my time in martial arts, I have had the honor of receiving "Instructor of the Year" through the Illinois Taekwondo Association in 2012, and ""Master Instructor of the Year" through the United States Martial Arts Hall of Fame in 2022. My passion for martial arts and teaching others drove me to open Coffman's Martial Arts in 2011, and it is now the largest martial arts school in Southern Illinois.


At Coffman's Martial Arts Academy, we view each student as a member of the family. With that said, we desire commitment, a hard work ethic, and a strong will to succeed. Through training, each student is taught our 5 tenents: courtesy, integrity, perseverance, self-control, and indomitable spirit. We will strive to help each one of our students achieve success as they mature in the arts.
